About the Program

Ideas are everywhere; finished products are rare. If your next big app idea is stuck in a doc instead of trending on Product Hunt, this program is for you. We combine solo-builder speed with team-grade infrastructure to help you go from prototype to paying customers fast. Using the latest AI-native tools (Claude Code, Lovable, Bolt, and more), we accelerate development so you can ship meaningful features in hours, then scale them with repeatable processes for launch, support, and monetization. At MSquare Automation Solutions we already build custom apps for clients starting at $5,000+. This initiative teaches you the same playbook so you can monetize your ideas and ship with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Anyone with an app idea - developers, founders, entrepreneurs, students. No coding experience required.

Who owns the app after you build it?

You retain 100% ownership of your app idea and source code. We're here to help you validate and build it.

What does Co-build mean?

There are several ways you can work with us:

Option 1: You build, we guide. If you plan to do the majority of the building yourself but have a few questions along the way, join our Master AI Automation Community community for just $1/month to get your queries answered via chat.

Option 2: You build, we collaborate. If you are handling the build but need our direct suggestions over a call or screenshare, join our Master AI Automation Community community at the $17/month pricing tier.

Option 3: We build it for you. If you want us to build the entire app, submit your requirements and we will assess the complexity to provide custom pricing.

Who bears AI tool subscription and infrastructure cost?

The participant.

What's the deadline to submit?

June 8th, 2026.

Can I submit multiple ideas?

Yes, but focus on your strongest idea. We recommend submitting your best concept.

Do I need to be a developer?

No! Business owners, founders, and non-technical people are welcome. We handle the technical building.

What if I want to hire you for the full build after the webinar?

We'll discuss pricing and timeline based on your app's complexity post the webinar. No obligation.

Will you steal my idea?

No. We sign NDAs if needed. Your idea is yours. We've built 100+ apps for clients and have a reputation to protect.

Can I leave Master AI Automation Community any time?

Yes, anytime. But you'll want to stay (we have great content).

Will I get recording of the webinar?

No, we encourage you to attend live.
